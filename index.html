OUR HERITAGE

Slow Brewed. Lovingly Handcrafted.

Inspired by the authentic congee restaurants of Hong Kong, Bubur Inn focuses on the traditional art of slow-cooking congee. Our broth base is simmered for over 4 hours to achieve that velvety Cantonese texture that melts on your palate.

Paired with fresh, pan-seared dumplings packed with chicken and crunchy cabbage, our menu is kept simple and focused. We select each ingredient carefully to guarantee warmth, texture, and taste in every single serving.
